# WELCOME TO TURTLE CROSSING GAME

> In this game, we have a player character represented by the 
> turtle. We have cars represented by the different coloured 
> boxes which moves from right to left.

- You are supposed to cross the turtle, navigating past the 
traffic successfully without being hit to play as long as it takes.
- On crossing the road, you will be brought back into the starting
 line, with an increase in difficulty-sped up cars.
- If hit the GAME OVER.

## CONTROLS
**Use the "Up" arrow key to move the turtle across.**
