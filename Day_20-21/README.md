# Welcome to My Snake Game

The snake represented by a blue bar moves around the screen as
directed by the player/user.
The snakes movement can be controlled with 8 keys:

## How to Play
### Controls

**Arrow Keys**
```
      "Up" key to move up
      "Down" key to move down
      "Left" key to move sideway left
      "Right" key to move sideway right
```

**Letter Keys**
```
      "w" key to move sideway up
      "s" key to move sideway down
      "a" key to move sideway left
      "d" key to move sideway right
```

- The food for the snake represented by blue dot appears at random
on the screen.
- Your mission is, should you choose to accept is to score the highest
points possible by colliding with the food which lengthens the
snake and increases the score.
- Food disappears and reappears at random on screen when eaten.

## !!! ALERT !!!
Game restarts once the snake collides with the boundary of the
screen and/or the snake collides with any part of its body.
